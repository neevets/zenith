# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone participating in Zenith. Regardless of your level of experience, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, or religion, we aim to maintain an environment where talent and collaboration come first.

---

## Expected Behavior

To maintain a healthy community, we expect all contributors to:

* **Be empathetic:** Remember that behind every line of code or comment there is a person.
* **Accept criticism gracefully:** Constructive *feedback* is what makes Zenith faster and more secure.
* **Focus on the common good:** Always prioritize what is best for users and the stability of the language.
* **Use inclusive language:** Avoid unnecessary jargon or language that may exclude others.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Under no circumstances will we tolerate:

* Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
* *Trolling* behavior or repeated provocations that disrupt the workflow.
* Publishing private information about others without their explicit consent (doxing).
* Any form of sexual harassment or unwanted attention.

---

## Enforcement and Reporting

Zenith maintainers have the authority and responsibility to remove or edit any comments, code, or contributions that do not comply with these guidelines.

**Have you seen something inappropriate?**  
Please report it directly to: **conduct@zenith-lang.org**

Every report will be reviewed confidentially, promptly, and fairly to ensure the community remains an excellent place to build the future of programming.
